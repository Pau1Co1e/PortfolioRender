About Me

Hello! I’m Paul Coleman, an AI and Machine Learning Engineer with a strong foundation in computer science and a passion for solving complex problems across multiple domains. While my primary focus is on advancing AI and ML technologies, I am particularly interested in their applications within the financial and cybersecurity sectors.

My journey began at Utah Valley University, where I earned a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science. Throughout my academic career, I’ve built up a solid track record in software engineering, algorithm development, and robotics, with hands-on experience in machine learning through my university internship. This experience introduced me to computer vision and natural language processing, laying the groundwork for my future endeavors in AI/ML. I’m committed to exploring diverse opportunities in AI/ML, whether in finance, cybersecurity, or beyond. To learn more about my work, including key projects and experiences, head over to the projects page.
